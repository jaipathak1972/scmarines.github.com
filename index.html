About Us

COMPANY BACKGROUND: ﻿

SCHELP MARNINE .  was founded in the year 2023 by Mariners ENGINEER and professionals from Shipping, Offshore and Dredging Industry. They graduated from one of the world’s most renowned Maritime Training Institute . After graduation, they acquired a combined sailing experience of more than 25 years on various types of vessels sailing world-wide. After stepping ashore they jointly gained experience of more than 20 years in various fields related to shipping, such as off-shore services, dredging, technical / crew management at global level, ship chartering, commercial operations etc. They have professionally managed and handled Bulk Carriers, Tankers (Crude, Product and Chemical), Containers, Self unloaders, Trans-shippers, OSVs, PSVs (DP1, DP2, DP 3), Dredgers, Multi-utility crafts etc.
